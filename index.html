DAILY PROTOCOLS

  • Observe tank daily for signs of nutrient deficiencies or excess.
  • Regular water changes are crucial for removing waste and replenishing trace elements.
  • Always treat new water with a dechlorinator like Prime before adding it to the tank.
  • Use Stability when starting a new tank, after water changes, or when adding new fish.
  • It's easier to add more nutrients than to remove an excess. Start with recommended doses and adjust slowly.
